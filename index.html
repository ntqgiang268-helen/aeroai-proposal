05 / The Gap

What Existing Tools Don't Cover

Most AI tools are built for the cloud. Edge is a different game — and those tools consistently fall short in the same four ways.

Cloud training ≠ Edge performance

A model trained for cloud inference often fails or runs slowly on edge hardware. Different chips require different optimization — and most tools don't do that for you.

No video → annotation pipeline

One 10-minute video = thousands of frames. Generic annotation tools make you extract and label them one by one. That's hours of work before you even start training.

No pre-deployment test environment

Most workflows have no way to validate model performance on an edge-like environment before going live. You find out it fails in production — not before.

Hardware lock-in

Switch from NVIDIA to Intel? In most tools, that means rebuilding your entire deployment pipeline. Every. Time. Aero AI is hardware-agnostic by design.

These gaps are the norm — not the exception. Aero AI was built specifically to fill them.
